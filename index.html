How to Play Dreadwood

The Goal

Welcome to the Dreadwood, a haunted frontier stalked by rival vampires! Choose one of four unique factions (like the orderly Sheriffs or chaotic Outlaws) and lead your pair of bloodsuckers to dominance. Your objective is to outlast the competition and be the final faction surviving on the board.

Your Turn

Each turn, you get Action Points (AP) to spend commanding your two vampires. Use these points thoughtfully for movement, attacks, and setting traps. Once you've used your AP, your turn ends, and the next player goes.

Basic Actions

  • Pivot (1 AP): Change the direction your selected vampire faces to prepare your next move or shot.

  • Bloodwells (ü©∏): Your faction's lifeline. If an enemy destroys all three, your faction is eliminated!
  • Hazards: Tombstones & Dynamite block shots & throw paths (Dynamite explodes!). Black Widow webs stop movement. Grave Dust curses those who land on it.
  • Cursed (Purple Ring): Slowed (max 1 move/turn), cannot Shoot/Throw. Cure by moving onto a clear square adjacent to ANY Bloodwell.

Winning

Eliminate rivals by destroying all their Bloodwells OR taking out both of their vampires. Last faction standing wins!
